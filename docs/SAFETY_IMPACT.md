# 🛡️ Safety Impact Analysis

SENTINEL-Φ transitions plant safety from a **Reactive** to a **Predictive** paradigm.

## Key Human Impact Metrics

### 1. 17-Minute Lead Time Advantage
Traditional SCADA systems trigger alarms *after* a gas threshold is breached. SENTINEL-Φ analyzes momentum (Gas + Shift Fatigue + Active Permits) to predict a breach **17 minutes** before it occurs, providing ample time for safe, orderly evacuation.

### 2. Elimination of "Normalization of Deviance"
By tracking the **Safety Culture Score** dynamically over time, SENTINEL-Φ prevents organizations from slowly accepting declining safety standards. If multiple near-misses occur, the system forces management intervention.

### 3. Near-Miss Memory (ChromaDB RAG)
Human operators forget past accidents. SENTINEL-Φ maintains an active vector database of every historical fatality. When current conditions mirror a past disaster, the Intelligence Layer flags it instantly.

### 4. Flawless Emergency Execution
During an emergency, panic causes communication failures. SENTINEL-Φ's execution layer immediately dispatches multilingual audio payloads (English, Hindi, Telugu) to the exact affected zones, eliminating confusion.
